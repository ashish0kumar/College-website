Welcome to NSUT

One of India's most reputed University in the domain of technical education.
The institute has grown leaps and bounds since its inception, both in size and in the scope of its activities.
The overall academic environment in the institution is highly conducive for achieving excellence in education and research.
It has been consistently ranked as one of the top engineering colleges in India and has featured prominently in various
national level surveys conducted by reputed professional and trade magazines.

Courses Offered

Our Campuses

The main campus of NSUT is located in Dwarka, Delhi. It is a spread over an area of 145 acres of land.
Govt. of NCT Delhi has expanded the NSUT in 2020 further into two more campuses namely East Campus (Formerly Ambedkar Institute of Advanced Communication
Technologies & Research) at Geeta Colony and West Campus (Formerly Ch. Brahm Prakash Government Engineering College) at Jaffarpur.

Our Facilities

A wide of range of facilities have been provided to the students to ensure a conducive learning atmosphere as well as a pleasant stay in campus.
NSIT ensures that various aspects of student welfare have been well taken care of.

Central Library

Central Library of Netaji Subhas University of Technology is functioning from 1st & Mezzanine Floors of Library Building. Library Building is centrally situated.

Sports

NSUT has a range of facilities including a football ground, cricket ground, basketball court, lawn tennis court, volleyball court, and indoor gymnasium. There is also a running/jogging track on the campus.

Food & Canteens

In addition to the regular mess facilities in the hostels, the NSUT campus boasts of affordable canteens spread throughout the campus. All canteens provide delicious food at reasonable prices.
